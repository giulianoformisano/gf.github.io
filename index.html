Welcome and thank you for visiting!

I am a PhD Candidate in Social Data Science at the Oxford Internet Institute, University of Oxford, as an ESRC and Nuffield College Scholar.

My research focuses on computational text analysis and political polarisation, using machine learning, natural language processing, network analysis, and time-series.

Alongside my PhD, I work as a Computational Social Science Researcher at Oxford’s Development Institute and Department of Politics, and Science Po's MediaLab.

Previously, I completed an MPhil in Politics (Oxford, 2021) and a BA in Politics and International Relations (Exeter, 2019).
